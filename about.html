About GROCEMAC

  1. Home
  2. About GROCEMAC

Aiming for a green future with sustainable energy being accessible to everyone, GRÜNIQ was founded and based in Germany. Inevitably, soaring energy prices make it more lucrative to switch to renewable energy and benefit from our infinite energy resource; the sun. We at GRÜNIQ share a common passion which actively supports the energy transition for a clean future. Driven by innovative ideas, we are working progressively on the development of advanced products. Thus, our focus is on sustainable energy products that are based on efficient energy generation and storage systems. Since customer satisfaction and sustainability are GRÜNIQ's ultimate goals, we assign great importance to high-quality and durable products. We are committed to creating energy independence which thereby enables everyone to contribute in a greener future that provides a tomorrow-worth living for our children.

Founders & Team

Solar energy is by no doubt gaining prominence these days considering the shift towards sustainable solutions in all fields. The idea of solar energy bloomed in our company after 16 years of dedication in the manufacturing industry and deeply connecting with consumers' needs. Therefore, we built brands founded on trust, loyalty and professionalism.

The business and engineering backgrounds of the company’s owners inspired the passion behind green energy. We are seeking to preserve our environment through the manufacturing of high-quality products that rely on renewable energy which in turn proves to be financially advantageous on the long run.
